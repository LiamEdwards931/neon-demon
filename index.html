About Us

We are a small tattoo studio located in the heart of Wednesbury.


Our artists have years of experience with custom creative designs.


We only use top of the range inks and equipment.


We are dedicated to making your new tattoo perfect! please stop by or contact us for a free consultation.


Our styles range from but aren't limited too:

  • Mandalas
  • Photo-realism
  • Japanese
  • Old School
  • Anime
  • Cartoons

Why Come To Neon Demon Tattoo?

You Dream It We Ink It!

Our talented team of artists will take your ideas and transform them into a piece of art you will cherish forever!


We know sometimes that getting a tattoo can be scary and painful, (trust me we know) that's why we sell numbing cream in the shop so you can have your tattoo and not worry so much about the pain.


Our artists have a variety of different styles and specialities, get in touch or pop down to the shop to get a free consultation about your ideas!
